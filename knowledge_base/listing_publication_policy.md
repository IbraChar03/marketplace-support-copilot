# Listing Publication Policy

Document ID: KB-LISTING-001
Version: 1.0

## Required listing data

Every used vehicle listing must include:

- vehicle identification number;
- readable VIN photo;
- registration document;
- sale price;
- make, model and registration year;
- at least six exterior and interior photographs.

## Listing status

A listing can have one of these statuses:

- `draft`: the dealer is still preparing the listing;
- `pending_review`: documents or information are being verified;
- `published`: the listing is visible on the marketplace;
- `rejected`: the listing does not satisfy publication requirements.

## Missing VIN photo

A used vehicle listing without a readable VIN photo must be rejected.

The dealer must upload a readable VIN photo before requesting another review.

## Missing registration document

If the registration document is missing or waiting for verification, the listing remains `pending_review`.

The dealer must upload the document before publication.

## Review time

A complete listing is normally reviewed within 24 hours.

A manual review can require up to 48 hours.
